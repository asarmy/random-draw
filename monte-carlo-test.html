Random Draw Generator Validation

Monte Carlo sampling is used to validate the random sampling approach by computing the distribution of Tops (A,B,C) and Bottoms (D,E,F) from 10,000 draws.

The most likely combinations are a 3 top / 2 bottom or 2 top / 3 bottom. The least likely combinations are a 0 top / 5 bottom or 5 top / 0 bottom. The distribution of 0/5, 1/4, 2/3, 3/2, 4/1, 5/0 is expected to be normal (Gaussian).

The histogram shows the expected distribution is recovered with the random draw generator.
